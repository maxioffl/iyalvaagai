Cloth Bag Movement

The path to find this World and this Universe in its true state by the course of Love is what our forefathers have taught and left us with. In this nature, from the blades of the grasses that hold the dew to the stars way beyond our reach, there is a lot to learn, experience and to revel in joy every moment of we knew where to look. On this continuous path, Iyalvaagai travels on spreading native seeds, creating existential spaces for self sustainability, creating awareness about natural farming, setting up practical libraries for children, creating vegetable farming grounds, conducting nature walks, forming book clubs to introduce various books about the environment and so on.. Wanting to take the farmers beyond the cultivational lands, Iyalvaagai began a humble attempt in the form of an open organic market called 'Namma Ooru Sandhai' where the farmers get to sell their products directly to the consumers, and the people get to meet the hands farming their food. Iyalvaagai requests and will be glad to host all the people who wish and strive for the happiness and betterment of this world as a society.

Services


Cloth Bag Movement

Promoting the usage of cloth bags

Introducing materials as an alternative to plastic

Giving cloth bags as complimentary gifts

Creating Awareness about plastic and the usage of cloth bags



Inner Services

Bringing together farmers and Creating a space for them to sell their products

Using alternatives for plastics to pack the products

Boost the local economy by promoting the sales of locally produced products



Organic Farmers Market

A space for making the farmers and the consumers meet

Since the farmers belong to the local area, there is an opportunity to visit the farm land directly

Ensuring the reliability and trustability of the products



Childrens Library

Weekly free classes for training silambam and parai

Conducting training camps for students during holidays and winter vacation

Setting up vegetable gardens and terrace gardens at student houses and schools

Training the students to participate in sports events at block, district and state level
